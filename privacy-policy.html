A Practical Explanation of Website Data Handling

Growwise Urban is an informational and educational website focused on urban farming, indoor growing, hydroponics, equipment planning, and related growing topics.

The website does not require visitors to create an account or submit payment information. Personal information may still be provided voluntarily through the contact form or generated through ordinary website operation.

01

Scope and Acceptance

This section explains where the policy applies and how it relates to your use of the website.

This Privacy Policy applies to information processed through the Growwise Urban website, including its public pages, growing guides, legal pages, contact form, cookie preference interface, and related website functionality.

In this policy, “Growwise Urban,” “we,” “us,” and “our” refer to {{companyLegalName}}. “Website” refers to the Growwise Urban website and the pages operated under the Growwise Urban brand.

By using the website, you acknowledge that you have reviewed this Privacy Policy. Your use of the website is also subject to the Terms of Service and Cookie Policy .

Informational Website

Growwise Urban provides general educational content. The website does not create a professional, consulting, agricultural, medical, legal, or commercial-service relationship solely because a visitor reads content or submits a general inquiry.

Information Outside This Policy

This policy does not control the independent practices of external websites, hosting providers, content delivery networks, email systems, browser providers, device manufacturers, internet service providers, or other third parties that operate under their own privacy terms.

Cookies, Local Storage, and Similar Technologies

Browser storage may be used to support essential functions and remember website preferences.

Cookies are small text files that websites may place on a device through a browser. Local storage is a browser feature that can retain website-specific information on the device.

Growwise Urban may use browser storage for purposes such as:

  • Remembering a cookie-consent preference.
  • Supporting essential interface behavior.
  • Maintaining basic security and anti-abuse functionality.
  • Preventing repeated display of the same preference notice.

You can usually remove or block cookies and local storage through your browser settings. Doing so may cause preference notices to reappear or affect certain website functions.

Children’s Privacy

The website is intended for a general audience and is not directed specifically to young children.

Growwise Urban does not knowingly request personal information from children under 13 through the contact form.
